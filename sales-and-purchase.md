# **Odoo 16 Sales & Purchase - Comprehensive Assessment Project**

## **Project Title: "Global Electronics Distributor - End-to-End Trading Operations"**

### **Company Overview**
ElectroDistribute Inc. is an electronics components distributor with:
- Global operations in 3 regions: Americas, EMEA, Asia-Pacific
- 5000+ SKUs across categories: Semiconductors, Passive Components, Connectors, Power Supplies
- 200+ suppliers worldwide
- 1000+ B2B customers including OEMs, Contract Manufacturers, MRO customers
- Multiple sales channels: Direct sales, E-commerce, Retail partners

## **Real-World Business Scenarios**

### **Scenario 1: Multi-Channel Order Management**
**Situation:** Customer orders through e-commerce, requests modification via email, then calls sales rep
**Tasks:**
1. Receive web order for 1000 units of Component X
2. Customer emails request to increase to 1500 units
3. Phone call to expedite shipping
4. Process all changes maintaining single order record
5. Update inventory allocation

### **Scenario 2: Global Supply Chain Disruption**
**Situation:** Primary supplier in Asia faces production delays
**Tasks:**
1. Identify affected purchase orders
2. Find alternative suppliers in different regions
3. Calculate impact on landed costs
4. Update sales orders with new delivery dates
5. Communicate with affected customers

### **Scenario 3: Large Tender/Bid Management**
**Situation:** Government tender requiring complex quotation
**Tasks:**
1. Create tender with multiple line items
2. Configure special pricing for bulk quantities
3. Setup payment terms (30% advance, 70% on delivery)
4. Include warranty and service terms
5. Generate professional proposal document

### **Scenario 4: Customer Credit Issue**
**Situation:** Key customer exceeds credit limit but wants urgent delivery
**Tasks:**
1. System blocks order due to credit limit
2. Sales manager requests exception
3. Finance approval workflow
4. Temporary credit increase
5. Monitor payment closely

### **Scenario 5: Return & Exchange Process**
**Situation:** Customer returns defective goods, wants replacement
**Tasks:**
1. Create RMA authorization
2. Receive returned goods with inspection
3. Issue credit note or replacement
4. Update inventory (defective stock)
5. Analyze defect patterns
